By the FGC

For the FGC

Fight Klub provides a bridge for fighting game enthusiasts who seek game knowledge, discussion, ideas, and mechanics.

We have assembled all of the information from fighting game experts that a fighting game player would need to improve their skills and enter the competitive scene.

Our value to you, our customers, is to provide helpful guides and articles on how to get competitive at a variety of fighting games, have a forum for our customers to have discussions and give feedback, and run weekly fighting game tournaments to test your skills and provide a competitive experience.

Our core values include:

  • Competitive Experience – We value the competitive experience by providing smaller scale tournaments for our customers that will allow them to get experience in the competitive scene.
  • Helpful Content – We value helpful content by hiring fighting game experts to maximize the chance that our customers will become experts themselves.
  • Inclusivity – We value inclusivity by allowing anyone to join our community whether they are somewhat experience at fighting games or a complete novice.
  • Our Community – We value our community through our ability to incorporate ideas from our customers that will make our product exactly what our customers want.
  • Variety – We value variety by providing content on any fighting game with a current or projected competitive scene to allow fighting game enthusiasts of all types to find value in our product.
